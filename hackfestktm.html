About the hackathon

A hackathon is an event that gathers programmers and tech enthusiasts with different skill sets to “hack”, or work collaboratively, on a software project. Hack Fest Kathmandu is a platform where student and developer community come together to build innovative solutions that solve real problems.


  • Promote creativity & team working
  • Provide students with mentorship and guidance from professionals
  • Build innovative solutions
